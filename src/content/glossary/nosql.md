---
term: NoSQL
short: Non-relational databases that trade tables and joins for flexibility or scale; "not only SQL".
---

NoSQL ("not only SQL") is the umbrella for databases that don't organize data as
[[relational-database|relational]] tables — [[document-database|document]],
[[key-value-store|key-value]], [[graph-database|graph]], and others.

The movement arose in the mid-2000s to scale across many machines
([[horizontal-scaling]]) by relaxing relational guarantees. Since then the tools
have converged: many have added [[sql]]-like languages and
[[transaction|transactions]] back in.
