---
term: Database management system
short: The software that stores and manages a database and answers queries against it — e.g. SQLite, PostgreSQL.
---

"Database" means two things: the data itself, and the software that manages it —
properly a database management system, or DBMS. SQLite, PostgreSQL, MySQL, and
MongoDB are all DBMSs.

A [[relational-database|relational]] DBMS — the kind you query with [[sql]] — is
often called an [[rdbms]].
