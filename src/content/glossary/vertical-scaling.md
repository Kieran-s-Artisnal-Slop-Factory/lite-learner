---
term: Vertical scaling
short: Handling more load by using a bigger, more powerful single machine. "Scaling up."
---

Vertical scaling — "scaling up" — means running your database on a more powerful
single machine: more CPU, memory, and disk. It's simple and preserves strong
guarantees like [[transaction|transactions]], but one machine has a ceiling.

When that ceiling wasn't enough for the web-scale giants, they turned to
[[horizontal-scaling]] instead.
