---
term: Embedding
short: A list of numbers representing the meaning of text, an image, or audio, so similar things sit close together.
---

An embedding turns text, images, or audio into a long list of numbers, positioned
so that similar meanings land near each other. "Puppy care" and "dog training" end
up close; "Paris travel" far away.

A [[vector-database]] stores embeddings and finds the nearest matches to a query,
which is how modern semantic search and AI retrieval work.
